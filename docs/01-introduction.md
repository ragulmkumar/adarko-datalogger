# Introduction

Welcome to the **ADARKO Datalogger** mobile application! This professional-grade app is designed to help you manage and maintain your ADARKO datalogger devices through seamless Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) connectivity.

## What is ADARKO Datalogger?

The ADARKO Datalogger app serves as your central hub for interacting with compatible BLE datalogger devices. These devices are specialized sensors and data collection units that gather environmental or operational data and transmit it wirelessly.

## Main Purpose

The primary function of this app is to:

- **Discover** nearby datalogger devices using Bluetooth
- **Connect** securely to your devices
- **Configure** device settings for optimal performance
- **Update firmware** to ensure your devices have the latest features and security improvements

## Key Features

- **Device Discovery**: Quickly scan and find nearby datalogger devices
- **Secure Connection**: Establish reliable BLE connections with your devices
- **Configuration Management**: View and modify device settings including:
  - Network configuration (APN, server address, port)
  - Data transmission intervals
  - Device identification settings
- **Firmware Updates**: Perform over-the-air (OTA) firmware updates to keep your devices current
- **Real-time Status**: Monitor device connection status and operation
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive design suitable for both technical and non-technical users

## Supported Devices

This app is designed to work with ADARKO datalogger devices that support Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) communication. The app automatically detects compatible devices during the scanning process.

## Why Use This App?

- **Convenience**: Manage multiple devices from your smartphone
- **Reliability**: Stable Bluetooth connections with automatic reconnection
- **Security**: Secure communication protocols protect your data
- **Updates**: Keep your devices up-to-date with the latest firmware
- **Monitoring**: Track device status and configuration in real-time

## Getting Started

To begin using the ADARKO Datalogger app, simply install it on your Android device and follow the setup instructions in the next section. The app will guide you through granting necessary permissions and connecting to your first device.

---

*Note: This application requires Android 8.0 or later and Bluetooth Low Energy support on your device.*
