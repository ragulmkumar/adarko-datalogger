# Installation

This section guides you through installing the ADARKO Datalogger app on your Android device.

## System Requirements

Before installing the app, ensure your device meets these requirements:

### Android Device Requirements
- **Operating System**: Android 8.0 (API level 26) or later
- **Bluetooth**: Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) 4.0 or higher support
- **Storage**: At least 50 MB free storage space
- **RAM**: Minimum 2 GB RAM recommended

### Hardware Requirements
- Bluetooth-enabled Android smartphone or tablet
- Stable internet connection (for firmware downloads and updates)

## Installation Steps

### Step 1: Download the App
1. Open the Google Play Store on your Android device
2. Search for "ADARKO Datalogger"
3. Locate the official ADARKO Datalogger app
4. Tap "Install"

### Step 2: Grant Installation Permissions
- If prompted, allow the installation from unknown sources (if downloading from a website)
- The app will request permission to install on your device

### Step 3: Wait for Installation
- The installation process typically takes 30-60 seconds
- You'll see a progress indicator during installation

### Step 4: Open the App
- Once installed, tap "Open" from the Play Store
- Or find the ADARKO Datalogger icon on your home screen or app drawer
- The app icon features a blue Bluetooth symbol

## Required Permissions

The ADARKO Datalogger app requires certain permissions to function properly. You'll be prompted to grant these during first use:

### Bluetooth Permissions
- **Bluetooth Scan**: Allows the app to discover nearby BLE devices
- **Bluetooth Connect**: Enables connection to datalogger devices
- **Location Access**: Required for Bluetooth scanning on Android (fine location)

### Storage Permissions
- **File Access**: Allows selection of firmware files for updates

### Why These Permissions Are Needed

| Permission | Purpose | When Used |
|------------|---------|-----------|
| Bluetooth Scan | Discover nearby datalogger devices | During device scanning |
| Bluetooth Connect | Establish connection to devices | When connecting to a device |
| Location (Fine) | Enable Bluetooth scanning | Required by Android for BLE operations |
| File Access | Select firmware update files | During OTA firmware updates |

## Post-Installation Setup

After installation:

1. **First Launch**: The app will initialize Bluetooth services
2. **Permission Prompts**: Grant all requested permissions when prompted
3. **Bluetooth Enable**: Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your device
4. **Device Ready**: Your device is now ready to scan for datalogger devices

## Troubleshooting Installation

### Installation Fails
- **Cause**: Insufficient storage space
- **Solution**: Free up at least 50 MB of storage and try again

### App Won't Open
- **Cause**: Device compatibility issues
- **Solution**: Ensure your Android version is 8.0 or later

### Permissions Denied
- **Cause**: Permissions not granted during setup
- **Solution**: Go to Settings > Apps > ADARKO Datalogger > Permissions and grant required permissions
